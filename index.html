The Story Behind
The Machine

I've always been more connected to machines than to people. Before AI was a buzzword, I was building systems — trying to understand how intelligence could be encoded into code.

Two years ago I built my first forex bot. It was rough. But it started something. I kept going, kept debugging at midnight, kept asking harder questions until the architecture was right.

EKG Empire is what happened when I refused to stop. A live Deep Reinforcement Learning trading engine — custom environment, custom reward shaping, regime detection, live execution. Built alone. From Lagos. With no institutional backing.

Now I'm relocating to Canada with my wife, building the portfolio that proves what's possible when constraints become creative parameters.

What The

EKG Empire
Sovereign DRL Trading Engine
● Live Deployed

A fully autonomous trading engine that learns from the market rather than following rules. Built with a custom Gymnasium environment, Sharpe-normalized reward shaping, and an ADX regime filter — the agent sees TREND vs CHOP every candle and adapts through learned behavior, not hard-coded logic. 5M training steps across 8 parallel environments.
